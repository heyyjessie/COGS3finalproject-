About

My Journey ⁺☀︎₊˚

My parents immigrated from China to Canada in search of job opportunities and a better life for their children. Thus, I was born in a city next to the Rocky Mountains and known for its cowboys: Calgary, Alberta. I lived there for 17 years and expected to stay for the rest of my life. I applied to UCSD on a whim and was pleasantly surprised by the acceptance offer. Now, I am an international student studying general biology by the beautiful Pacific Ocean shores of La Jolla.

My Passion ₊˚⊹♡

I have a deep passion for biology and medicine, and I am committed to staying informed about the latest developments in these fields. I first discovered my passion for biology while taking the International Baccalaureate program in high school. I fell in love with conducting experiments and discussing findings. After volunteering in the health field, I realized how inspired I was by medicine. Now, my passion has led me to volunteer at a hospital and take on many biology teaching roles during my time at UCSD. Please view my resume for more details.

My Interests ˚୨୧⋆｡

Outside of my passion for biology and medicine, I have a deep love for art. I enjoy drawing on my iPad and sharing my creations, which has garnered me a Pinterest following of almost 90K.
